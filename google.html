About Google OAuth Scopes

Google OAuth Scopes are the permissions your app requires from a user. These scopes are displayed to the users (google users) to grant your app access to their account. Inside the hood, these permissions grant your app access to the google APIs listed below. With these APIs you read or make changes to users account. For example, with Google OAuth APIs you can write mails (gmail) on behalf of a google user. You can also delete mails on behalf of users. Learn more here
